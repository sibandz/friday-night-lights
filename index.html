Regulations

RULES & GUIDELINES

Please review all rules before registering. Coaches and team managers are responsible for communicating these to players.

General Tournament Rules
  • Event starts at 10:00 AM
  • All games will start and end on a central siren.
  • Score sheets to be handed in. Please ensure score sheets have age group and houses CLEARLY marked.
  • Umpires and referees are arranged for all matches.
  • Teams will have 1 minute to vacate the playing area after the match before the next matches start.
  • Congratulatory handshakes to be done after the match outside the playing area.
Football Rules (U7/U8/U9)
  • Matches will be 8 minutes one way.
  • Boys to wear TAKKIES as they are playing on the astro.
  • Shin pads are compulsory.
  • 7-a-side with rolling subs.
  • All squad members must play at least one full game.
  • U9 - Point allocation: 3 for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss.
  • If points are equal at the end, it will come down to amount of goals scored.
  • All other rules of football will apply, and the ref's decision is final.
Football Rules (U11/U12/U13)
  • Matches will be 7 minutes each way with a 1 minute half-time.
  • U11 will play 9-a-side with rolling subs.
  • U12 and U13 will play 11-a-side with rolling subs.
  • All squad members must play at least one full game.
  • Point allocation: 3 for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss.
  • If points are equal at the end, it will come down to number of goals scored.
  • All other rules of football will apply, and the ref's decision is final.
Netball Rules
  • Matches will be 10 minutes each way with a 2 minute half-time.
  • 7-a-side with rolling subs.
  • All squad members will play at least one full game.
  • Point allocation: 3 for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss.
  • If points are equal at the end, it will come down to number of goals scored.
  • All other rules of netball will apply, and the ref's decision is final.
