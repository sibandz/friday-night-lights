Regulations

RULES & GUIDELINES

Please review all rules before registering. Coaches and team managers are responsible for communicating these to players.

General Tournament Rules
  • Umpires and referees are arranged for all matches.
  • Teams will have 1 minute to vacate the playing area after the match before the next matches start.
  • We request that teams do the congratulatory handshakes after the match outside the playing area.
  • There is no entry fee for the event. See you there!
Football Format: Fusion 5
  • Team SizeInfield players: 4, Goalkeeper: 1, Reserve: 1.
  • Match LengthTwo halves of 5 minutes each, with a 1-minute interval in between.
  • Game RulesRolling substitutions can be made at any stage during the game; there is no need to wait for a timeout.
  • ScoringGoals can be scored from anywhere on the turf. Standard rules: All normal FIFA rules will be followed.
  • Power PlayThe final two minutes of the match will be a power play format where all goals scored secure double points.
Netball Format: Flash 5
  • Team SizeTotal players: 6 players. On court: 5 players on the court at all times.
  • ShootersGA (Goal Attack) and GS (Goal Shooter) are the only players who can shoot. There are different shooting zones, meaning teams can score up to three points with any one shot.
  • Match LengthTwo halves of 5 minutes each, with a 1-minute interval in between.
  • Game RulesRolling substitutions can be made at any stage during the game; there is no need to wait for a timeout.
  • ScoringGoals outside of the shooting circle: 3 points. Shoot in the outer circle: 2 points. Score under the post: 1 point.
  • Power PlayThe final two minutes of the match will be a power play format where all goals scored secure double points.
Hockey Format: Fantastic 5
  • Team SizeInfield players: 4, Goalkeeper: 1, Reserve: 1.
  • Match LengthTwo halves of 5 minutes each, with a 1-minute interval in between.
  • Game RulesRolling substitutions can be made at any stage during the game; there is no need to wait for a timeout. Restrictions: Lifting of the ball is not permitted.
  • ScoringGoals can be scored from anywhere on the turf. Standard rules: All normal FIH (International Hockey Federation) rules will be followed.
  • Power PlayTeams select when they want to make use of this, and it lasts for two minutes. During this time, all goals scored secure double points, meaning the tables can turn from winning to losing, or vice versa, very rapidly. Each team is only allowed one Power Play during a fixture.
